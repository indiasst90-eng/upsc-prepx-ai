# Story 7.1: Answer Writing - Submission Interface

**Epic:** 7 - Practice & Evaluation - Answer Writing & Essays
**Story Number:** 7.1
**Status:** Draft
**Created:** December 23, 2025
**Sprint:** Sprint 8 (Epic 7)
**Estimated Effort:** Medium (M)
**Risk Level:** LOW

---

## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** a dedicated interface to practice Mains answer writing with timed mode,
**so that** I can simulate real exam conditions and improve my writing speed.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. UI page: /practice/answers with question selection (daily questions, topic-based, PYQ)
2. Question display: shows question text, word limit (150/250 words), syllabus topic, difficulty
3. Writing interface: rich text editor with word counter, timer display, save draft functionality
4. Timed mode: countdown timer based on word limit (150 words = 10 min, 250 words = 15 min)
5. Auto-save: saves answer draft every 30 seconds to prevent data loss
6. Submit button: "Submit for Evaluation" sends answer to AI scoring pipeline
7. Database: `answer_submissions` table with user_id, question_id, answer_text, word_count, time_taken, submitted_at
8. Question bank: displays 5 daily questions (1 per GS paper), refreshes daily at midnight
9. History: "My Submissions" tab shows past answers with scores and feedback links
10. Entitlement: Free users get 2 evaluations/month, Pro users get unlimited
