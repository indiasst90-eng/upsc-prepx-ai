# Story 6.6: Revision Bundle - 10-Minute Quick Quiz

**Estimated Effort:** Medium (M)
**Risk Level:** LOW

---

## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** each weak topic to have a 10-minute timed quiz,
**so that** I can test my understanding immediately after revision.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Quiz generation: for each revision target, generate 10 MCQs (4 options each)
2. Question sourcing: 50% from PYQ database (if available), 50% AI-generated new questions
3. AI generation: Edge Function calls A4F LLM with prompt "Generate 5 UPSC-style MCQs for {topic} with 1 correct and 3 plausible distractors"
4. Difficulty mix: 3 easy, 5 medium, 2 hard questions
5. Database: `revision_quizzes` table with user_id, topic_id, questions_json, created_at
6. Timed mode: 10-minute countdown timer, auto-submits when time expires
7. Instant scoring: shows score (X/10) immediately after submission
8. Answer explanations: displays correct answer with 2-3 sentence explanation for each question
9. Performance tracking: updates user's topic confidence score based on quiz result
10. Retry: users can retake quiz after 24 hours (to prevent answer memorization)
