# Story 6.10: Weekly Progress Video Briefing

**Epic:** 6 - Progress Tracking & Personalization
**Story Number:** 6.10
**Status:** Draft
**Created:** December 23, 2025
**Sprint:** Sprint 7 (Epic 6)
**Estimated Effort:** Large (L)
**Risk Level:** MEDIUM

---

## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** a personalized weekly video summary of my progress,
**so that** I can reflect on my accomplishments and adjust my strategy.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Video generation: every Monday morning, generate 2-3 minute personalized video for each active user
2. Content includes: topics completed last week, quiz scores, confidence gains/losses, streak status, upcoming targets
3. Script generation: Edge Function retrieves user's weekly stats and generates narrative script
4. Manim visualizations: includes animated charts showing confidence trends, time spent per subject, quiz performance
5. Revideo assembly: combines narration (user's preferred TTS voice), Manim charts, transitions, motivational messaging
6. Video storage: saves to Supabase Storage at `progress-videos/{user_id}/week_{week_number}.mp4`
7. Database: `progress_videos` table with user_id, week_number, video_url, stats_json, created_at
8. Notification: sends push notification "Your weekly progress report is ready!" with link to /progress
9. UI: /progress page displays current week's video with play controls and download option
10. Motivational AI: script includes personalized encouragement based on performance ("Great job improving Polity by 15%!" or "Let's focus on Economy this week")
