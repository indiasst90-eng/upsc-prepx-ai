## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** an instant video debrief after my mock interview,
**so that** I can review my performance and improve.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Debrief generation: triggered immediately after interview ends
2. Analysis: LLM reviews transcript, identifies strengths and weaknesses
3. Video structure:
   - Performance summary (2 min)
   - Best answers highlighted with suggestions
   - Areas for improvement with examples
   - Suggested reading/resources
4. Manim visualizations: confidence meter, topic-wise score chart
5. Duration: 5-8 minutes
6. Render time: <5 minutes
7. Delivery: notification "Your debrief is ready"
8. Archive: saved to interview history
9. Share: optional share with mentor for human review
10. Comparison: compare with previous interview scores
