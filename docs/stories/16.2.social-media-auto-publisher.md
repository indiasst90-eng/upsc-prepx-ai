## Story

**As an** admin,
**I want** to automatically publish content to social media platforms,
**so that** I can promote the platform and reach more UPSC aspirants.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Platform support: YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, Telegram
2. Content types:
   - Daily CA video (YouTube, Telegram)
   - Topic shorts (Instagram, YouTube Shorts, Twitter)
   - Weekly documentary (YouTube)
3. Auto-publishing: schedule posts when content is generated
4. Platform formatting:
   - Instagram: 9:16, hashtags, caption
   - Twitter: 280 chars, video link
   - YouTube: title, description, tags, thumbnail
   - Telegram: text summary + video link
5. OAuth integration: connect platform accounts
6. Draft mode: review posts before publishing
7. Analytics: track views, engagement, shares per platform
8. Scheduling: queue posts for optimal times
9. Team collaboration: multiple admin accounts
10. Compliance: add platform-specific disclaimers
