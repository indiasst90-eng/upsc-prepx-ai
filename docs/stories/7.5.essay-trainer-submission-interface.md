# Story 7.5: Essay Trainer - Submission Interface (1000 words)

**Epic:** 7 - Practice & Evaluation - Answer Writing & Essays
**Story Number:** 7.5
**Status:** Draft
**Created:** December 23, 2025
**Sprint:** Sprint 8 (Epic 7)
**Estimated Effort:** Medium (M)
**Risk Level:** LOW

---

## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** a dedicated interface to practice 1000-word essays,
**so that** I can prepare for the Essay paper with proper structure and depth.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. UI page: /practice/essays with essay topic selection (contemporary, philosophical, abstract)
2. Topic categories: Philosophy & Ethics, Social Issues, Economy, Science & Tech, Environment, Governance
3. Writing interface: rich text editor with word counter (target 1000-1200 words), timer (90 minutes)
4. Essay structure guidance: sidebar shows recommended structure (Introduction 100w, Body 700w, Conclusion 200w)
5. Auto-save: saves draft every 60 seconds
6. Outline mode: optional pre-writing outline section where users can jot down main points
7. Database: `essay_submissions` table with user_id, topic_id, essay_text, word_count, time_taken, outline_json, submitted_at
8. Weekly topics: platform provides 3 fresh essay topics every Monday
9. Topic difficulty: tags essays as Easy/Medium/Hard based on abstraction level
10. Entitlement: Free users get 1 essay evaluation/month, Pro users get 4/month
