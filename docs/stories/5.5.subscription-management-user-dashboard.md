# Story 5.5: Subscription Management - User Dashboard

**Epic:** 5 - Monetization & Subscription System
**Story Number:** 5.5
**Status:** Draft
**Created:** December 23, 2025
**Sprint:** Sprint 6
**Estimated Effort:** Medium (M)
**Risk Level:** MEDIUM

---

## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** to view my current subscription, billing history, and manage renewals from my profile,
**so that** I can control my subscription and access invoices.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Subscription page: /settings/subscription with current plan card
2. Plan details: features, usage stats (doubts used, videos generated)
3. Billing history: table with date, amount, invoice, status
4. Manage subscription: Change Plan, Cancel Subscription buttons
5. Change plan: upgrade/downgrade with prorated calculation
6. Cancel flow: retention offer, cancels at period end (not immediately)
7. Renewal toggle: enable/disable auto-renewal
8. Payment method: display last 4 digits, Update button
9. Status indicators: Active (green), Cancelled (yellow), Expired (red), Trial (blue)
10. Support link: Contact support opens chat/email
