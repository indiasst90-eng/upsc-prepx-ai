## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** to customize the AI teacher's voice, speed, and teaching style,
**so that** I can learn in a way that suits my preferences.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Voice options: male/female voices with different accents (Indian English, American)
2. Speed control: 0.75x to 1.5x playback speed
3. Style presets:
   - Professor: formal, detailed
   - Mentor: warm, encouraging
   - Peer: casual, relatable
4. Custom settings: save preferences to profile
5. Preview: listen to sample before saving
6. Consistency: same voice used across all videos
7. Premium voices: celebrity-style voices for Pro users
8. Voice cloning: upload 1 minute of speech for personalized voice (optional)
9. Integration: applies to all TTS-generated content
10. Accessibility: optimized for different hearing preferences
