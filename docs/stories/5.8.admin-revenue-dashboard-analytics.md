# Story 5.8: Admin Revenue Dashboard & Analytics

**Epic:** 5 - Monetization & Subscription System
**Story Number:** 5.8
**Status:** Draft
**Created:** December 23, 2025
**Sprint:** Sprint 6
**Estimated Effort:** Medium (M)
**Risk Level:** LOW

---

## Story

**As an** admin,
**I want** a comprehensive revenue dashboard showing MRR, subscriptions, churn, and trends,
**so that** I can make data-driven business decisions.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Revenue dashboard: /admin/revenue with key metrics cards
2. MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue): current MRR, MRR growth %, trend chart
3. Subscriptions: active count, new this month, churned, churn rate %
4. Trial metrics: trials started, trial-to-paid conversion %, avg trial length used
5. Revenue breakdown: by plan (Monthly, Annual), by channel (organic, referral, coupon)
6. Charts: revenue over time (line), subscriptions by plan (pie), churn rate trend
7. Cohort analysis: retention by signup month (table)
8. Top coupons: most used, highest revenue generated
9. Export: download revenue report as CSV or PDF
10. Real-time updates: dashboard refreshes every 5 minutes
