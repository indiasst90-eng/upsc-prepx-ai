## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** to earn XP, badges, and maintain streaks for my learning achievements,
**so that** I stay motivated and track my progress.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. XP system:
   - Video watched: 10 XP
   - Quiz completed: 20 XP
   - Doubt asked: 15 XP
   - Notes generated: 25 XP
   - Streak day: 50 XP bonus
2. Badges:
   - First Steps: complete first topic
   - Week Warrior: 7-day streak
   - Quiz Master: 100% on 10 quizzes
   - Syllabus Explorer: visit all GS papers
   - Consistent: 30-day streak
3. Streaks: consecutive days with at least 30 minutes study
4. Subject rooms: 3D virtual rooms per subject (Polity, History, etc.)
5. Progress bar: level progression with visual indicator
6. Milestones: celebrate levels with confetti, badge unlock
7. No competitive leaderboards (per Business Vision)
8. Self-comparison: "You vs Last Month" metrics
9. Analytics: personal dashboard showing XP history, trends
10. Opt-out: users can disable gamification
