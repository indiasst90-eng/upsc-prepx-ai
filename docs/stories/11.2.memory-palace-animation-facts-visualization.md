## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** to convert lists of facts into visual memory palace animations,
**so that** I can remember information through spatial memory techniques.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Input: list of facts/points (e.g., "Articles of Indian Constitution")
2. Palace selection: predefined themes (library, museum, courtroom, classroom) or custom
3. Mapping algorithm: distribute facts across 5-10 "rooms" or "stations"
4. Visual elements: each fact gets a unique visual representation
5. Animation flow: virtual walk through palace, stopping at each station
6. Scene types:
   - Room entrance (2s): establish location
   - Station stop (10-15s): fact appears with visual
   - Transition walk (2s): move to next station
7. Total duration: 60-90 seconds per 10 facts
8. Spaced repetition: scheduled reviews at 1 day, 3 days, 7 days, 14 days
9. User customization: rearrange stations, change visuals
10. Export: download as video for offline review
