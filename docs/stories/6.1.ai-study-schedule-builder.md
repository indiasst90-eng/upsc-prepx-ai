# Story 6.1: AI Study Schedule Builder - Adaptive Planning

**Epic:** 6 - Progress Tracking & Personalization
**Story Number:** 6.1
**Status:** Draft
**Created:** December 23, 2025
**Sprint:** Sprint 7 (Epic 6)
**Estimated Effort:** Large (L)
**Risk Level:** HIGH (Complex algorithm)

---

## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** an AI-powered study schedule that adapts to my exam date, current progress, and weak areas,
**so that** I can optimize my preparation time and cover the syllabus systematically.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Schedule builder page: /schedule with exam date input, target hours/day
2. AI analyzes: syllabus completion %, weak topics, time until exam
3. Generates daily schedule: topics to study, time allocation, revision slots
4. Schedule adapts: updates weekly based on actual progress
5. Calendar sync: export to Google Calendar, iCal format
6. Task breakdown: daily tasks with checkboxes (read notes, watch video, solve PYQs)
7. Rest days: recommends 1 rest day per week
8. Alerts: daily reminder notification for scheduled tasks
9. Progress tracking: mark tasks complete, schedule auto-adjusts
10. Schedule saved: accessible from /schedule, shows next 7 days by default
