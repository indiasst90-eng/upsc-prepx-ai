## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** AI-powered predictions of topic difficulty and exam weightage,
**so that** I can prioritize my study effectively.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Prediction model: analyze historical PYQ data (2010-2024)
2. Difficulty scoring: 1-10 scale per topic based on:
   - Past question frequency
   - User performance data (aggregate)
   - News signals (current relevance)
3. Weightage prediction: expected questions from topic in upcoming exam
4. Confidence score: show reliability of prediction
5. Visualization: difficulty heatmap on syllabus navigator
6. Time recommendation: "Spend 5 hours on this topic" based on difficulty
7. Trend analysis: topics becoming more/less important over time
8. Alerts: "This topic is trending up in PYQs, prioritize it"
9. Export: download prediction report as PDF
10. Updates: model retrains weekly with new data
