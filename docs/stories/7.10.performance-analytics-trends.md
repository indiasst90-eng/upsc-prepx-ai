# Story 7.10: Test Series - Performance Analytics & Trend Graphs

**Epic:** 7 - Practice & Evaluation - Answer Writing & Essays

**Status:** Draft
**Created:** December 23, 2025
**Sprint:** Sprint 8 (Epic 7)
**Estimated Effort:** Medium (M)
**Risk Level:** LOW

---

## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** visual analytics showing my performance trends across multiple tests,
**so that** I can track improvement over time and identify persistent weak areas.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Analytics dashboard: /practice/analytics displays comprehensive performance graphs
2. Score trend: line graph showing scores over time for last 10 tests
3. Subject-wise performance: radar chart showing strength across different subjects (Polity, History, Economy, etc.)
4. Accuracy trends: tracks accuracy % over time to identify improvement or decline
5. Time management: average time per question trends, identifies if user is too fast/slow
6. Comparison mode: compare current test performance with previous attempts
7. Weak areas identification: automatically flags subjects with <50% accuracy consistently
8. Strong areas: highlights subjects with >75% accuracy for confidence boost
9. Export: users can download analytics report as PDF with graphs and insights
10. AI insights: generates text summary "Your Economy performance has improved by 15% in last month. Focus more on Environment."
