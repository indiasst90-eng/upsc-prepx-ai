# Story 5.10: Referral Program - User Acquisition

**Epic:** 5 - Monetization & Subscription System
**Story Number:** 5.10
**Status:** Draft
**Created:** December 23, 2025
**Sprint:** Sprint 6
**Estimated Effort:** Medium (M)
**Risk Level:** LOW

---

## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** to refer friends and earn rewards when they subscribe,
**so that** I can share the platform and get benefits.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Referral page: /referral with unique referral code (6-char alphanumeric)
2. Referral link: https://upsc-prepx.ai/signup?ref=ABC123
3. Tracking: when referred user signs up, referrer_id saved in user_profiles
4. Reward: when referred user subscribes, referrer gets 1 month free extension
5. Referral dashboard: shows referred users count, subscribed count, rewards earned
6. Leaderboard: top referrers displayed (gamification)
7. Social share: quick share buttons for WhatsApp, Twitter, Email
8. Terms: max 10 referrals rewarded per month (prevent abuse)
9. Fraud detection: IP/device fingerprinting to prevent self-referrals
10. Admin analytics: referral conversion rate, viral coefficient (K-factor)
