# Story 5.6: Pricing Page - Plan Comparison & CTA

**Epic:** 5 - Monetization & Subscription System
**Story Number:** 5.6
**Status:** Draft
**Created:** December 23, 2025
**Sprint:** Sprint 6
**Estimated Effort:** Small (S)
**Risk Level:** LOW

---

## Story

**As a** UPSC aspirant,
**I want** a clear pricing page comparing all plans with features and benefits,
**so that** I can make an informed decision on which plan to purchase.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Pricing page: /pricing with 4-column comparison table
2. Plans: Free, Pro Monthly (₹599), Quarterly (₹1499, save 16%), Annual (₹4999, save 30%)
3. Features listed: video doubts, daily CA, notes, search (with Free limits vs Pro unlimited)
4. Highlight Annual: "Most Popular" badge, best value messaging
5. CTA buttons: "Start 7-Day Trial" (if not used), "Subscribe Now" (if trial used)
6. FAQ section: common questions (refund policy, cancellation, features)
7. Testimonials: 3-5 user testimonials with photos, success stories
8. Comparison toggle: monthly vs annual pricing view
9. Mobile-optimized: horizontal scroll for table, stacked cards on small screens
10. A/B testing: track conversion rates by pricing page variant
