## Story

**As a** premium UPSC aspirant,
**I want** to experience 360° immersive visualizations of historical events and geographical locations,
**so that** I can learn through immersive cinematic experiences.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. 360° content: historical battles, geographical features, cultural sites
2. Platform support: WebXR for VR headset compatibility, mobile 360 viewing
3. Interactive hotspots: click to learn more about specific elements
4. Embedded quizzes: test knowledge during/after experience
5. Navigation: look around, zoom in/out, move between scenes
6. Audio: spatial audio with narration
7. Duration: 5-15 minutes per experience
8. Subjects: History (battles, monuments), Geography (mountain ranges, rivers)
9. Quality: 4K resolution, 60fps
10. Pro feature: only for Pro/Annual subscribers
